FAQ

Why are there *'s on the Statistics Yes's?

The differences are:
Supporter includes: current viewer count, average watch time, total amount of viewers
Broadcaster + Exclusive Partner: All of supporter, hot times (when most people were watching), graph of viewership


What is 'Exclusive Partner'?

Good question! An Exclusive Partner of Bracketify will receive multiple on-site benefits (such as unlimited coins) as well as direct support, home page shoutouts, discount codes for viewers, and more. The only thing EPs need to do is follow our agreed terms (and be exclusive to Bracketify).


How much is 'Exclusive Partner'?

Pricing is based on multiple factors of the company and our agreed terms that will be discussed when you hit "Contact". Pricing can be anywhere from $9.99/mo to $99+/mo, it all matters on our agreed terms.


Are there returns?

In general, there are no returns (though you can cancel your membership at any time and still pay for the current month). The 'but' is if the charge was unauthorized. If you have a case of unauthorized transactions, please immediately contact billing@bracketify.com and we will resolve the issue.
