FAQ

Why are there *'s on the Statistics Yes's?

The differences are:
Supporter includes: current viewer count, average watch time, total amount of viewers
Broadcaster + Partner includes: All of supporter, hot times (when most people were watching), graph of viewership


What is the difference between Broadcaster and Partner?

Good question! Broadcaster is a flat $49.99 a month, while if you become a partner you will need to agree to our Partnership Terms which will be discussed and then sent to you after we finalize terms. Note: Partnerships are not 100% free (nor legally binding (though if you breach our agreement and don't provide a valid reason after 2x offenses, you may be terminated)), though based on agreed terms will usually be at least 50% off Broadcaster pricing)


Are there returns?

In general, there are no returns (though you can cancel your membership at any time and still pay for the current month). The 'but' is if the charge was unauthorized. If you have a case of unauthorized transactions, please immediately contact billing@bracketify.com and we will resolve the issue.
